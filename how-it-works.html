What to Expect

Session Format

Sessions are 1-on-1, online or in-person. Each one follows the plan with a clear goal. Not just "what homework do you have tonight?"

Between Sessions

Your child gets custom worksheets to practice. If they get stuck, the daily drop-in is there so they don't have to wait until next week.

Communication

If something changes at school, like a new unit or a big test, let us know. We keep the plan flexible so it always matches what your child needs.
