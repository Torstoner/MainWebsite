Why Stone Sharp

We built this program to fix the problems we kept seeing with how tutoring usually works.

What Makes Us Different

Structured System

Every student gets a clear plan. Sessions follow a set order. We find the weak spots and work on them one step at a time.

Custom Worksheets

We make practice sheets from scratch for each student. Your child works on what they need. Nothing is one-size-fits-all, and nothing is wasted.

Daily Drop-in Support

Students don't have to wait until next week for help. We have daily online sessions so they can get help right away between their regular tutoring times.

Tutor Oversight & Standards

We don't let just anyone tutor here. We pick our tutors, train them, and check their work. That way, every student gets the same high quality.

Our Tutor Standards

We don't hire just anyone. Every tutor at Stone Sharp Academy is picked because they know math well, can explain things clearly, and show up on time.

Once they join us, our tutors follow our system. They don't make up their own way of teaching. That means your child gets the same great experience no matter which tutor they work with.

We watch over every tutor to make sure sessions stay on track. We check that the plan is being followed and that your child is getting the help they were promised.
