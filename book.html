What to Expect

The assessment is a short meeting, usually 20-30 minutes, where we learn about your child's situation and find where the gaps are.

  • We'll talk about your child's current course and grade
  • We'll find the specific areas where they're struggling
  • We'll go through questions to check for gaps in the basics
  • We'll share what we found and suggest next steps
  • There's no pressure and no commitment

After the assessment, you'll have a clear picture of where your child stands and what a plan to help them would look like.
