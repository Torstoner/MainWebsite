Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from parents about how Stone Sharp Academy works.

Programs start at $270 per month. This includes weekly 1-on-1 tutoring sessions, custom worksheets, daily drop-in sessions, and a clear improvement plan. Every student starts with a free assessment. No commitment needed to find out where your child stands.
We only teach Grades 9-12 math: Math 9, Math 10C, Math 20-1, Math 20-2, Math 30-1, and Math 30-2. All of our materials and plans follow the Alberta curriculum.
Both. We offer in-person tutoring in Edmonton and online sessions for students who like working from home. The quality is the same either way. The only difference is how you connect.
Drop-in sessions are daily online group sessions where students can get homework help, ask worksheet questions, or review for tests. They're included in every program at no extra cost. They run every day so students never have to wait until their next 1-on-1 session to get help.
We build worksheets from scratch based on your child's specific gaps. After the first assessment and after each session, we create practice that targets exactly what your child needs to work on. They're not pulled from a textbook. They're made just for your child.
The assessment is a short meeting where we learn about your child's current course, what they're struggling with, and their learning history. We go through a set of questions to find where the gaps are. After that, we share what we found and suggest next steps. There's no obligation. It's just about finding out where your child is right now.
It depends on where your child is starting and how far behind they are. Some students feel more confident within the first few weeks. Others, especially those with gaps from earlier grades, may take a bit longer. What we can say is that with regular attendance and practice, the plan makes a real difference.
Yes. There are no long-term contracts. If you need to pause or stop, just let us know. We believe the program should speak for itself. Not a contract.
Most tutoring is reactive. The tutor shows up and asks "what are you working on?" We do the opposite. Every student gets a plan based on their assessment, custom worksheets built for their specific gaps, and daily access to help between sessions. It's not freelance tutoring. It's a managed program with standards and a system built to get results.
Between 1-on-1 sessions, your child gets custom worksheets and daily online drop-in sessions. The worksheets help them practice what was covered in their last session. The drop-ins are there for quick help if they get stuck. The goal is to keep learning going between meetings, not just during them.
