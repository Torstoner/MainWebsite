About Stone Sharp Academy

We know what it feels like to watch your child struggle with something that used to come easily. The worry before report cards. The homework fights. We built this for families like yours.

Our Mission

By the time most families reach out, it's not about one bad test. It's months of confusion building quietly. What these students need isn't someone to re-explain the textbook. They need someone to test what they actually know, find the gaps, and build a plan around fixing them, with the right practice and daily support so they don't fall behind again.

That's what Stone Sharp Academy was built to do. We only teach Grades 9–12 math because doing one thing well matters more than doing a little of everything. We know the Alberta curriculum inside and out. Our whole approach is built to help students close real gaps and build real confidence.

Our Approach to Math Education

Structure Over Improvisation

Every session has a purpose. Before we teach anything, we figure out what's actually causing the problem. A student struggling with equations might really have a gap in fractions from two years ago. We find that first.

Practice That Matches the Problem

Generic worksheets waste time. We build custom materials for each student so they practice exactly what they need to work on. Nothing more, nothing less.

Support Between Sessions

Students get stuck between sessions, not just during them. Daily drop-in sessions mean they can get help the same day they run into a problem.

Our Team Standards

We don't work like a tutor marketplace. Every tutor at Stone Sharp Academy is picked for their math knowledge, teaching skill, and dependability. Once they join the team, they follow our system. The same approach, the same standards, the same focus on quality.

This means your child gets the same great experience every time. If anything needs to change, like the tutor, the focus, or the speed, we handle it. You work with us, not a freelancer.
