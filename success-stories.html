Student Transformations

Every student starts in a different place. Here's how the plan helped.

Grade 11 Student

Math 20-1
Where they started Falling behind on sequences and series. Avoiding math homework completely. Confidence was very low going into the second unit.
Where they ended up Felt better about tests. Started doing practice on their own. Went from struggling to keeping up with the class.

With a clear plan and custom worksheets that focused on the basics, this student started building confidence one step at a time.

Grade 10 Student

Math 10C
Where they started Had trouble with basic algebra and linear functions. Had been falling behind since junior high. Parent was worried about them getting even further behind.
Where they ended up Built steady study habits. Went from failing to passing. Started showing up to drop-in sessions on their own.

By finding gaps from junior high and working on them step by step, this student finally had a solid base to build on.

Grade 12 Student

Math 30-1
Where they started Needed a high mark to get into university but kept getting uneven results on exams. Understood the ideas in class but couldn't show it on tests.
Where they ended up Built a solid test-taking plan. Got more steady results on timed practice. Felt ready going into the diploma exam.

Custom worksheets with exam-style questions helped this student go from understanding the material to actually doing well on tests.

Grade 11 Student

Math 20-2
Where they started Had completely given up on math. Was just trying to pass. Parent said they were "checked out."
Where they ended up Got interested in math again. Started asking questions during drop-in sessions. Finished the course with a passing grade and new confidence.

Sometimes the problem isn't ability. It's not having a plan. With a clear plan and daily help, this student found a reason to try again.
